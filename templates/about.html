About

At the heart of AI Story Teller lies a transformative perspective: the considerate use of natural language prompts as a programming language for our large language models. This unique methodology uncloaks a new dimension of interaction and control over AI technologies like GPT-4.

The AI Story Teller intertwines the disruptive intellect of GPT-4, inspiration garnered from ElevenLabs at MIT, and the dynamic innovation of Stable Diffusion. Conjoining these technologies into an intricate fabric, the platform generates narratives that immerse readers in an extraordinary storytelling experience.

Natural language prompts used here reflect the platform's commitment to assimilating a programming language into day-to-day human language. The prompts encapsulate instructions to guide GPT-4 into crafting the desired narratives. These prompts, written in an easily understandable language, function as a bridge to control and direct the AI. Such an approach is something that echoes the evolution of programming itself - from the rigidness of machine language to the fluidity of high-level programming languages.

While the platform's robust internals rely on Python, Flask, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, it's the integration of natural language as a source code that has enabled it to tread new technological grounds. Delving into the technicalities, you can visit our open-source repository on GitHub. To understand the power of such prompts, you can check them out here.

AI Story Teller is the brainchild of Jorin Eggers. His innovative approach has essentially redefined how we understand and control AI technologies. Through this platform, Jorin has showcased the marvelous possibilities that come with the fusion of artificial intelligence and human-like storytelling.
